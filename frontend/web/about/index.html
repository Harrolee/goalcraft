The idea

Most goal apps hand you someone else's checklist. Screen Test starts with identity — the person you're deciding to be — and lets you define the handful of metrics that actually make that real. Every entry you log is a small vote for that self.

How it works

Declare a goal and the identity behind it. Add your own metrics — songs released, sessions attended, whatever counts as evidence — with targets that matter to you. Log as you go and watch the ledger fill. Optional nudges keep you honest between the big days.

Who it's for

Anyone chasing something specific and self-defined — artists, builders, athletes, founders — who'd rather measure the work than repeat affirmations.
