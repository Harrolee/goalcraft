Privacy Policy

Effective 8 July 2026

Screen Test helps you define who you're becoming and track the metrics that prove it. We collect as little as possible, we never sell your data, and you can delete everything at any time.

What we collect

  • Account. When you sign in with Apple or Auth0, we store an identifier and (if you share it) your email address, to give you a private account.
  • Your goals & metrics. The goals, identities, metrics, targets, and the entries you log. This is the content you create.
  • Basic technical data. Standard server logs (timestamps, error diagnostics) to keep the service running and secure.

What we do not collect

No advertising identifiers, no location, no contacts, no third-party analytics or ad tracking. We do not sell or rent personal data to anyone.

How we use it

Only to operate Screen Test: to store and display your goals and metrics, sync across your devices, and—if you turn them on—send you reminder notifications. Notifications are scheduled on your device; disabling them stops them immediately.

Where it lives

Your data is stored in a managed PostgreSQL database. Authentication is handled by Apple and/or Auth0. These providers process only what's needed to sign you in.

Deleting your account

Open the app → the ⋯ menu → Settings → Delete Account. This permanently and irreversibly removes your account and all associated goals, metrics, and entries from our database. You may also email us to request deletion.

Children

Screen Test is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

If this policy changes we'll update this page and revise the date above.
