Frequently asked

How do I add a metric?
On the dashboard, tap ＋ Metric under “The Ledger,” give it a name, an optional target, an icon and colour, then add it.

How do I log progress?
Tap the gold ＋ on any metric to add one, or open a metric and use Mark It Down to log a larger amount with a note.

How do I delete my account?
In the app, open the ⋯ menu → Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and all goals, metrics, and entries. Prefer we do it? Email us.

How do reminders work?
In Settings, turn on “Let him leave you notes.” Reminders are scheduled on your device and stop the moment you turn them off.
